Job Description

What’s it all about?

This is a high-visibility opportunity for a technical leader who wants to work on large-scale problems with tangible business impact.

In this role, you will lead a small team of talented Data Scientists who support Visa’s Functions and Product (F&P) departments by answering key business questions with data-driven answers and actionable recommendations.

As a leader in the F&P Data Science team, you will:

Bring structure to ambiguous business problems, using computational and statistical techniques to translate them into highly scalable solutions
Set the standard for engineering excellence and shape the way we design, deploy, and maintain these solutions
Foster a data-driven culture across the entire company, enabling F&P departments to consume your insights and take measurable actions
Provide thought leadership and guidance within your team and across the broader Visa Europe Data Science community

What We Expect From You, Day To Day

Strategic Leadership

Set the team’s strategic direction and plan of execution to drive meaningful business results
Be a strong leader who can apply Data Science methods towards answering key business questions with data-driven answers
Influence F&P teams by translating data and insights into actionable recommendations and ensuring they are adopted to successfully deliver business outcomes
Stakeholder Leadership
Plan and communicate with partners to scope projects, agree timelines, and define responsibilities
Establish and foster a data-driven culture and strong relationships across F&P
Collaborate effectively with other Data Science teams across Visa
Team Leadership
Facilitate project execution and knowledge sharing, allowing the team to operate at a high level
Support the professional growth of a team of highly skilled Data Scientists
Model Visa’s Leadership Principles and create an inclusive and supportive environment where every employee can thrive

Technical Leadership

Be an experienced mentor to more junior Data Scientists on computational/statistical methods and their applications to loosely defined business problems
Demonstrate a product mindset by identifying, developing, and maintaining reusable components that can be deployed into productised solutions
Advance the team’s engineering craftsmanship by devising and sharing best practices
Develop a deep understanding of Visa’s data assets and how they can be used to support F&P teams across the company
Identify and prototype novel methods, algorithms, and tools from the open-source community and the scientific literature

Qualifications

What we're after...

Required Qualifications

Strong statistical knowledge and proven expertise in applying statistical solutions to business problems
Solid relational modelling (SQL) and programming skills (preferably in Python)
Good working knowledge of data visualisation / BI platforms such as Power BI or Tableau
Experience leading the delivery of large-scale data solutions using Big Data and distributed processing platforms such as Hadoop, Hive and Spark
Familiarity with modern DevOps practices (including Git and CI/CD)
Long-term strategic thinking and strong problem-solving skills
Stakeholder management ability including effective prioritisation, clear and concise communication, and delivery of actionable data-driven insights

Preferred Qualifications

A higher degree (or equivalent experience) in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or other quantitative discipline
Functional knowledge of cloud computing platforms such as AWS, Azure or GCP
Background in payments or banking
Experience creating and protecting Intellectual Property through patents
