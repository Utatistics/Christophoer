As a data scientist focusing on user growth efforts, you will have the opportunity to collaborate closely with a variety of teams within the User Growth Department, including product, operation, marketing, and algorithm teams. You will be able to leverage your data analytical expertise and strong business acumen and work with the UG country manager to accelerate the growth of Tiktok Shop business in UK.

Responsibilities
- Provide end-to-end quantitative analysis including metrics design, model development, data analysis, and dashboard building;
- Design and analyze AB experiments to optimize the efficiency and ROI of UG initiatives to constantly improve long-term customer value and ensure sustainable growth of the business;
- Apply user label and user portrait data assets and build synergy with data product teams to craft audience targeting capabilities of e-commerce performance marketing or event scenarios;
- Understand the UK Market landscape and major competitors, providing actionable insights to help achieve the growth of both business results and user scale.

Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree or above in STEM subjects. - 2.1 or equivalent achieved;
- Proficiency in SQL/Excel/R/Python/Tableau. Familiar with common statistical methods and applications (A/B testing, probability, regression);
- Have interest in e-commerce and big data, proven data analysis skills;
- At least 3 years of working experience in an analytical role involving 3 of the below: e-commerce/user growth/product optimization/business analytics/performance marketing;
- Have excellent verbal and written communication skills;
- Self-driven, able to work under pressure, and have the resilience to overcome difficult challenges.
