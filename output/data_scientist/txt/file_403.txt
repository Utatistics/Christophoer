Job Summary

DESCRIPTION

The Emerging Technologies & Intelligent Platform (ETIP) portfolio of AWS Professional Services helps customers unlock value from data using technologies such as IoT, AI/ML and Data Analytics

AWS EMEA (Europe, Middle East and Africa) Professional Services team is looking for an experienced ML practitioner who can apply innovative Machine Learning techniques to real-world problems and help customers accelerate use of ML in production with MLOps best practices. You will get to work in a team dedicated to advancing Machine Learning solutions at AWS and converting it to business-impacting solutions.

ETIP consultants take ownership of customer business outcomes; focusing on performance, scalability, management, and serviceability of products through assembling, creating and sharing effective content and best practices. You will work closely with colleagues in Professional Services Country/region leadership, Account Managers, Specialist Sales, Solutions Architects

This is a customer facing role. You will be required to travel to client locations to deliver professional services when needed.

Key job responsibilities
Use machine learning, data mining and statistical techniques to create new, scalable solutions for business problems
Analyze and extract relevant information from business data to help automate and optimize key processes
Design, develop and evaluate highly innovative models for predictive learning and accelerate their usage in production with ML Ops
Establish scalable, efficient, automated processes for large scale data analyses, model development, model validation and model implementation
Research and implement novel machine learning and statistical approaches

Basic Qualifications
An MS/PhD in CS, Machine Learning, Operational research, Statistics or in a highly quantitative field.
Strong foundations in ML Ops and helping customers deploy ML in production at scale
Extensive industry experience in predictive modeling and analysis, predictive software development
Working knowledge of programming languages, such as Java, C/C++, Python and/or R and use of ML libraries, such as scikit-learn, caret, mlr, mllib
Experience in mentoring junior team members, and guiding them on machine learning and data modeling applications
Strong communication and data presentation skills
Preferred Qualifications
Experience handling terabyte size datasets
Experience working with distributed systems and grid computing
Publications or presentation in recognized Machine Learning and Data Mining journals/conferences
