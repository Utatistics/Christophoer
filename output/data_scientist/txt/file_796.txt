About Our Client

Leading Housing Association

Job Description

This Leading Housing Association require a Data Scientist to join their Data and Technology Department to support the Corporate Services Directorate. As a Data Scientist, you will be responsible for using advanced data modelling techniques & technology to assist the business in making insights driven decisions. The role will be at the forefront of enabling organisational change to evolve innovation, analytics and digital capabilities.Key Responsibilities:
To find patterns and help solve problems faced by the organisation in innovative ways
To extract, analyse and interpret large amounts of data from a range of sources, using algorithmic, data mining, artificial intelligence, machine learning and statistical tools, to make it accessible to the business. Present all findings back to key business areas in a clear and engaging manner.
Advising business areas on any findings gleaned from utilising all available data sets and ensuring the Data environment is reliable, secure, cost efficient, and strategically sound
Working with and advising a multi-disciplinary team providing expert analysis and advice in a professional manner at all times irrespective of the complexity of the environment
Input into the overall management and development of data and develop the capability in the data science/insights area.
Work on a variety of projects across the whole of the business (HR, Finance, Operations etc) to:
Ensure colleagues spend more time using information rather than searching for it
Increase efficiency savings: better use of data leading to more informed decision-making, less paper, reduced duplication of information and process
Enhance Compliance with Government and regulator standards
Improve staff skills and competencies
Increase knowledge of stakeholders resulting in improved service and user satisfaction
Support the delivery of strategic and operational plans to time and within budget, supported by appropriate data architecture
Increase insight on supplier governance - building relationships with external parties and managing contracts.

Key Skills / Experience:
Bachelor's degree in mathematics, statistics, computer science or related field
Strong maths and analytical skills are essential to complete job requirements successfully
Experience operating as a Senior Data Scientist.
Knowledge of a variety of machine learning techniques (clustering, decision tree learning, artificial neural networks, etc.) and their real-world advantages/drawbacks.
Solid knowledge of various statistical techniques and experience using machine learning algorithms
Strong programming skills, especially R, Python and Java (C/C++ and Perl is desirable)
Demonstrated complex critical thinking and ability to creatively solve problems with measurable business impact
Able to compile and organise statistical information retrieved and present findings to senior leaders
Excellent stakeholder management skills at all levels, with strong written and oral communication skills.
Formal project management training and experience
Experience of commissioning services from third parties and managing relationships with suppliers and contractors
