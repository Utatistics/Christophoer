What You'll Do

In a world where Data will be critical for BCG's success, BCG has founded a new cross-functional Agile team, the Global Data Tribe, to create first-class, foundational, enterprise-wide Data capabilities and products to enable better decision-making, generate new insights, attain operational excellence, and minimize firm risk across all BCG functions and teams

The Global Data Tribe is looking for a Data Science Lead to drive the ideation, development, and implementations of data science & AI driven use cases across BCG business functions with specific high focus on People & HR business function. The individual will work with data tribe leadership to drive AI/ML adoption in BCG to drive value and help in shaping up this newly formed AI Center of Excellence as part of Global Data Tribe. Individual will work closely with functional areas stakeholders to end to end drive identification, development, implementation, and maintenance of variety of data science use cases across BCG globally with razor sharp focus to realize value from data science & AI.

The ideal candidates will have a customer-centric mindset and enjoy working with cross-functional teams to design and deliver exceptional data science solutions. They will have 6+ years of experience in providing analytics solutions to solve variety of real-life business problems and have delivered significant impact to organizations using ML & AI. They should have strong hands on data science technical capabilities coupled with very good communication skills & business thinking with proven experience in stakeholder engagement and coaching & leading people.

Among your responsibilities, you will:
Design and build analytics methodologies, solutions, and products to deliver value to BCG
Responsible to identify new use cases opportunities of Data science within business functions and work with stakeholders to quantify value and build concrete plan for execution
Work as an Individual contributor (IC) as well as lead data scientists on day-to-day basis on projects
Collaborate with other data engineers, product owners & team members within Data Tribe and with several stakeholders from functional areas across end-to-end project lifecycle
Own approach design, choice of tools & techniques, execution & deployment of solution, solution monitoring and regular enhancements
Own work plan, scope, stakeholders’ communication, and quality assurance across project lifecycle on day-to-day basis
Mentor & coach data scientists, senior data scientists, data engineers and other junior team members on need basis for a project
As the field of data science is rapidly evolving, you will be responsible for staying current on leading-edge business applications, tools and approaches, deployment & MLOps platforms and bringing them to life in BCG
