Job Description

Rakuten group has almost 100 million customer base in Japan and 1 billion globally as well, providing more than 70 services in a variety such as ecommerce, payment services, financial services, telecommunication, media, sports, etc.

Department Overview

AI Success Supervisory Department (AISSD) provides data science initiatives by leveraging Rakuten group’s data. The department has international culture created by excellent employees joined around the world and provides the cutting-edge data science. Following the strategic vision “Rakuten as a data-driven membership company”, AISSD is expanding our data activities across our multiple Rakuten group companies.

Data Science Consulting Department (DSCD)
Analytics Data Engineering Department (ADED)
Catalog Management Department (CAMD)
Enterprise Consulting Department (ECD)
