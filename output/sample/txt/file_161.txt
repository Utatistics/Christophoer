What you can expect:

Business Analytics & Insights (BAI) harnesses the power of data to drive robust analytical insights that inform some of Pfizer's most critical business questions. With colleagues across the globe, BAI’s rigorous analytical expertise is depended on as the compass and decision support for the enterprise.

The person in this role is accountable for leading a team of data scientists in the key International Developed Markets (e.g., EU5, Japan) to shape Pfizer’s go-to-market model through delivery of data-science driven strategic and tactical decision support. This leader will be responsibilities across multiple business units, including partnering with Biopharma country to understand strategic business needs, frame key issues and to ensure delivery of high-quality, data science services to these markets.

As a strategic partner to Medical and Commercial teams in these key markets, the person in this role will implement models and data-science derived insights that drive brands’ strategic priorities. This will include driving the execution and interpretation AI/ML models, framing problems, shaping solutions, and championing change. The successful candidate will join BAI colleagues worldwide that are constantly supporting business transformation through their proactive thought leadership, innovative analytical capabilities, and their ability to communicate highly complex and dynamic information in new and creative ways.
