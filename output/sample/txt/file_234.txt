Wunderman Thompson Tokyo is looking for Data Analyst who will work across exciting range of areas, interacting with other data team members, and closely working with the strategy, customer experience, technology, commerce and creative teams. This person will be reporting to the WT Tokyo Head of Data and Analytics.

Data Analyst is responsible for supporting the team on assigned projects and accounts. The position holder will work closely with other analysts and will support the data team in the development of data analysis, actionable insights, recommendations and reports for assigned client accounts.

What You’ll Do
Supporting client’s data requirements on all data-driven marketing activity
Identify, collect and analyse data from different sources to support clients data analysis including 1st party, 2nd party and 3rd party data.
Preparing and processing data and performing analytics based on client data
Collaborating with CX, Strategy, Tech, Account and Creative teams on all data related activities
Conducting pre and post campaign evaluations, including web analytics, Marketing ROI, engagement reporting, gap analysis and insights and hypothesis development
Driving deep market and consumer insights based on surveys, research, statistics, data analysis and other data techniques
Working on preparations of personas, profiles, segmentations, journey analytics, journey measurements, KPIs and measurements based on client and 3rd party data.
Prepare meaningful reports or dashboards based on data and research with insights and recommendations. That would include SEO and social media data analysis.
Assessing and reviewing databases and digital/martech platforms including quality, functionality, UX-centric usability, CRM-oriented design, and propose solutions
Taking on other assignments as assigned by the data director or head of data

Who You Are
Minimum of 3 years of experience in business with focus on data analytics, preferably gained in a digital or marketing environment
Good understanding of CRM and digital marketing including social media and email marketing
Experience of Adobe Experience Platform（Analytics/Target/Audience Manager）and understanding required details, such as tag management.
Experience of BI / visualization tools such as Tableau, PowerBI, Google Dat Studio, Domo
Knowledge of data science technologies such as R, Python, SQL
Knowledge of Marketing Cloud platforms such as Sales, Adobe is a plus
Strong analytical and statistical understanding
High ability to read and interpret data into useful content
Degree/Diploma in Marketing, Mathematics, Economics, Computer Science, Data Science, Statistics, or a similar qualification
Good written and verbal communication skills
Native level Japanese is required
Ability to understand written English, reply with simple English writing, and willing to learn and grow in the English-speaking environment
Excellent player as an individual as well as a positive influencer in a team.
Work in collaborative environment, team-oriented, communication skills and presentation skills.
